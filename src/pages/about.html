About Us

About The Journey Sober Coaching

At The Journey Sober Coaching, we believe in the power of human potential to heal and transform. Founded by Paul Sarris, our mission is to walk alongside you through all stages of sobriety—whether you’re just getting started or maintaining long-term recovery.

With 38 years of continuous sobriety and more than 12 years as a recovery and life coach, Paul brings lived experience, compassion, and professional expertise to help guide you on your journey. As a Certified Peer Recovery Coach and Recovery Coach Professional Facilitator, Paul is committed to supporting your spiritual, emotional, and physical growth.

“I know firsthand the strength, support, and guidance it takes to walk the path of recovery. My goal is to provide encouragement, accountability, and hope for anyone ready to embrace lasting change.”

Paul Sarris Founder & Certified Recovery Coach (CPRM, CPRC, NCFRC, RCPF)
