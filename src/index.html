About Us

About Road Tech Services

Road Tech Services was founded to make small engine repair more convenient for Michigan homeowners. No more loading heavy equipment into your truck — we come to you.

With certifications in Briggs & Stratton (Master Certified), Stihl (Silver Level), Kohler, and Kawasaki, our technician brings years of expertise directly to your driveway. Most issues can be resolved the same day if parts are available.

I’m Matt Olenzek, owner of Road Tech Services. After years of hands-on technical work, I built this business to save people time, money, and stress. My goal is simple: provide reliable, professional, and convenient equipment repair right at your home. Whether it’s your mower, snow blower, or generator, I’ll keep your equipment ready for every season.
