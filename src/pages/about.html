About Us

About Road Tech Services

Road Tech Services is a locally owned mobile repair business proudly serving homeowners across Michigan. We specialize in on-site repair and maintenance for lawn mowers, snow blowers, and generators, saving customers the hassle of transporting heavy equipment to a shop.

Our mission is simple: make small engine repair convenient, professional, and reliable. With expert certifications and same-day service available, we ensure your equipment is back up and running quickly — right in your driveway.

I’m Matt Olenzek, founder of Road Tech Services. As a Master Certified Technician for Briggs & Stratton, Silver-Level Certified in Stihl, and also certified in Kohler and Kawasaki, I’ve built this business to give homeowners an easier way to keep their outdoor equipment in peak condition. My goal is to deliver professional, same-day repair with honesty, care, and attention to detail.

Matt Olenzek Owner & Master Certified Technician
